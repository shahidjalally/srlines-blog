WABA stands for **WhatsApp Business Account**. It is the account layer inside Meta Business Manager used to manage numbers, message templates, and permissions for WhatsApp Business messaging.

## Why WABA matters

- It is required for WhatsApp Cloud API setup.
- It controls who can manage your messaging assets.
- It helps maintain compliance and account governance.
