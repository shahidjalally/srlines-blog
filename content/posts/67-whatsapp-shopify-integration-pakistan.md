WhatsApp Shopify integration in Pakistan helps merchants recover carts, confirm COD orders, and improve post-purchase communication. If implemented correctly, it can directly increase conversion and reduce support load.

## Why Shopify merchants need WhatsApp automation

Pakistani ecommerce stores often depend on COD and fast customer communication. WhatsApp performs well because it is immediate, conversational, and trusted.

Key outcomes include:

- Faster COD verification
- Lower fake order rate
- Better delivery communication
- Higher repeat purchase rates

## Integration workflow

## 1) Connect Shopify events

Sync key events into your WhatsApp system:

- New order created
- Order paid / pending COD
- Fulfillment updates
- Cart abandonment

## 2) Set up message templates

Create utility and marketing templates for:

- Order confirmation
- Shipment out for delivery
- Delivery delay notices
- Cart recovery offers

## 3) Build segmentation rules

Segment by first-time buyers, repeat buyers, high AOV customers, and abandoned carts with multiple sessions.

## 4) Add support handover

Route complex replies to human agents with context from Shopify order history.

## High-impact automation examples

- COD verification flow within 5 minutes of order placement
- 2-step cart recovery sequence at 30 minutes and 12 hours
- Post-delivery upsell after 7 days
- Win-back campaign after 45 days of inactivity

## Metrics that matter

- Cart recovery conversion
- COD confirmation success rate
- Delivery query deflection rate
- Repeat purchase within 60 days
