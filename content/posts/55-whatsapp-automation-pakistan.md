WhatsApp automation in Pakistan helps businesses handle repetitive conversations while keeping human support available when needed.

## Use cases

- Lead qualification for services and B2B.
- Cart recovery for e-commerce stores.
- Appointment reminders for clinics and education.

## Fast rollout plan

1. Start with one intent (new lead or support FAQ).
2. Add quick replies and template fallback.
3. Route high-intent users to a human agent.
4. Review weekly and optimize drop-off points.
