A WhatsApp CRM setup in Pakistan allows teams to centralize chats, assign owners, and sync every conversation with lead or customer records.

## Core benefits

- No missed leads across multiple agents.
- Better follow-up through pipeline stages.
- Cleaner reporting on sales and support outcomes.

## Implementation checklist

1. Connect WhatsApp inbox to your CRM.
2. Map fields (name, source, status, intent).
3. Add automation for tagging and routing.
4. Build follow-up reminders and escalation rules.
