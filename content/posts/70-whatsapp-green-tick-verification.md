WhatsApp green tick verification (official business account badge) can increase trust and improve brand credibility, especially for high-volume customer communication. But many businesses apply too early without meeting readiness criteria.

## What the green tick means

The green tick indicates WhatsApp has recognized your account as a notable, authentic brand. It is not guaranteed for every verified business.

## Eligibility foundations

Before applying, ensure you have:

- Fully verified Meta Business account
- Active WhatsApp Business API setup
- Consistent branded communication
- Strong domain and public brand presence

## How to improve approval chances

### 1) Strengthen brand signals

Maintain consistent brand identity across website, social profiles, and messaging assets.

### 2) Build media and web mentions

Notability is influenced by public visibility. Earn legitimate mentions in trusted publications and directories.

### 3) Maintain policy compliance

Accounts with poor quality or policy violations reduce approval probability.

### 4) Keep operational quality high

Respond quickly, avoid spammy campaigns, and maintain healthy message engagement.

## Common misconceptions

- Green tick is not a paid add-on.
- Verification alone does not guarantee badge approval.
- Small brands can apply, but notability evidence matters.

## Business impact

For many companies, a green tick can:

- Increase user confidence in outreach
- Improve response rates for key campaigns
- Reduce impersonation confusion

Treat green tick verification as part of a broader brand trust strategy, not a standalone growth tactic.
