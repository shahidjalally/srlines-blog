CTWA means **Click to WhatsApp Ads**. These ads open a WhatsApp conversation directly from Meta platforms, reducing friction between ad click and customer chat.

## Why brands use CTWA

- Generates high-intent inbound leads.
- Improves speed-to-conversation.
- Works well with automation and CRM routing.
