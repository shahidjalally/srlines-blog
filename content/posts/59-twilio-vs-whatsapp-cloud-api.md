Twilio and WhatsApp Cloud API both support business messaging, but they differ in control, cost structure, and architecture.

## Quick comparison

- **Twilio**: multi-channel convenience and managed abstractions.
- **Cloud API**: direct Meta integration and often better cost control at scale.

Choose based on your team’s channel strategy, technical bandwidth, and pricing model.
